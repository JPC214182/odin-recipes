Sweet and Spicy Beef

Description

This is a dish that is deeply savory, with a delightful interplay of sweet, salty, and spicy notes that linger on the palate.

Sweet and spicy beef is a dish that masterfully balances two fundamental and contrasting flavor profiles: the deep, rich sweetness of ingredients like brown sugar, honey, or a sweet chili sauce, with the fiery kick of chili flakes, Sriracha, or other hot peppers.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • A Sweetener
  • A Spicy Element
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pork Chops:
    • Pat the pork chops dry thoroughly with paper towels. This helps them get a good sear.
    • Season both sides generously with salt and black pepper.
  2. Heat the Pan:
    • Place a large skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat.
    • Add a tablespoon or two of cooking oil to the hot pan. You want the oil to be shimmering but not smoking.
  3. Sear the Pork Chops:
    • Carefully place the seasoned pork chops in the hot pan. Do not overcrowd the pan; cook in batches if necessary.
    • Sear for 3-5 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of your chops, until a golden-brown crust forms.
    • For thicker chops (1 inch or more), you might want to reduce the heat to medium after searing both sides and continue cooking for a few more minutes, flipping occasionally, until the internal temperature reaches 145 Fahrenheit
  4. Rest the Pork Chops:
    • Once cooked, remove the pork chops from the pan and place them on a plate or cutting board.
    • Allow them to rest for at least 3-5 minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat, resulting in a more tender and juicy chop.
  5. Serve:
    • Serve the pork chops hot.
