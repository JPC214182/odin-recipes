Garlic Shrimp

Description

As name suggested these shirmps are swimming together with garlics

Garlic Shrimp is a popular dish that features shirmp cooked in a rich, aromatic sauce primarily composed of garlic and butter or olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Shirmp
  • Garlic
  • Butter or Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Prepare Your Ingredients:
    • Thaw and pat the shrimp completely dry with a paper towel. This is crucial for a good sear.
    • Mince or thinly slice a generous amount of fresh garlic.
    • Chop your fresh parsley.
    • Have your butter, olive oil, and lemon ready.
  2. Season the Shrimp:
    • In a bowl, toss the shrimp with a pinch of salt and black pepper.
  3. Sauté the Garlic:
    • Heat a large skillet or pan over medium-high heat.
    • Add the olive oil and a tablespoon or two of butter.
    • Once the butter is melted and the oil is shimmering, add the minced garlic. Sauté the garlic for about 30 seconds to a minute, just until it becomes fragrant. Be careful not to burn it, as burnt garlic turns bitter.
  4. Cook the Shrimp:
    • Add the seasoned shrimp to the pan in a single layer.
    • Cook for 1-2 minutes per side, until the shrimp turn pink and opaque. Shrimp cook very quickly, so be sure not to overcook them, or they will become tough.
  5. Finish the Dish:
    • Turn off the heat.
    • Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the shrimp and stir.
    • Stir in the freshly chopped parsley.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve the garlic shrimp immediately, while hot. The rich, garlicky butter sauce is perfect for dipping with crusty bread or serving over rice or pasta.
