Vegetable Soup

Description

Vegetable soup is a comforting and hearty dish consisting of a variety of vegetables.

The foundation of the soup is typically a vegetable or chicken broth. This liquid is what carries and amplifies the flavors of all the ingredients. Some recipes may call for a tomato-based broth, while others are clear.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Liquid Base
  • Aromatics
  • Seasonings
  • Oil
