Fried Rice

Desciption

This Fried Rice recipe is easy to make but taste like you want it everyday at your breakfast.

Fried rice is a versatile and popular dish made by stir-frying cooked rice in a wok or frying pan with various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Aromatics
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Prepare Your Ingredients:
    • Make sure you are using cold, day-old cooked rice.
    • Whisk the eggs in a small bowl with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    • Chop any fresh vegetables you're using, like onions or scallions.
    • Chop any fresh vegetables you're using, like onions or scallions.
  2. Cook the Eggs:
    • Heat a little oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
    • Pour in the whisked eggs and quickly scramble them. Once cooked, remove them from the pan and set them aside.
  3. Saut√© the Aromatics and Vegetables:
    • Add a bit more oil to the hot pan.
    • Add your diced onions or garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Add any harder vegetables, like diced carrots, and cook for a minute or two. If using frozen peas and carrots, you can often add them a bit later.
  4. Add the Rice:
    • Add the cold rice to the pan. Use your spatula to break up any clumps.
    • Stir-fry the rice for several minutes, allowing it to heat through and slightly crisp up. The goal is to get each grain coated in the oil and separated.
  5. Combine Everything and Season:
    • Add the cooked scrambled eggs and any other vegetables (like peas or corn) back to the pan.
    • Pour in the soy sauce and continue to stir-fry everything together until the rice is evenly coated and has a consistent color.
    • Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. You can add a little more soy sauce or a dash of black pepper.
    • If using, stir in a touch of toasted sesame oil right before serving.
  6. Serve:
    • Garnish with the green parts of the scallions if you have them.
    • Serve the fried rice hot, either on its own or as a side dish.
